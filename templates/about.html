About JJARQK Technologies

JJARQK started simply as an idea between 6 friends. Throughout America, fast food has become a plague on the public's health. JJARQK wanted to create a solution.

The Creation of JJARQK Hunt

In order to reduce fast food consumption, JJARQK created JJARQK Hunt, a database of user submitted restaurants within the $15 per person price range. Currently, JJARQK Hunt is completely non-profit, all costs coming out of pocket. Also, JJARQK belives the privacy of its users is of utmost importance, so, we promise to never include advertizing in JJARQK Hunt.
